Home Goods

Beautiful Development

Branding, Packaging, Product Development, Photo Retouching, Illustration
2017-2019

As a designer at Gourmet Home Products, I am responsible for product development, branding, and packaging. I illustrated cute icons for kids hydration and worked on packaging for other bottles as well. Pattern making for the plastic sports bottles was one of my earliest projects as well as designing packaging for the corkcicles later featured on Nordstrom Rack's online store. Each project was completed within a few hours.

I worked with the lead product manager and president of my company to develop JADE bottles. Later on, I also photoshopped Instagram photos for our new brand. We did not have props nor a studio so I edited our new products onto hand picked flat lays we licensed from other artists. I worked with the marketing manager on this and provided website photos for jadebottles.com as well.

Our company specializes in a multitude of home goods and I was also responsible for creating two new brands for cleaning items. The logo and packaging (including the templates and dielines) for CLEAN+SIMPLE were completed in 1 day due to shipping deadlines. I photoshopped most of the photos in the packaging for Pure Care as well.

I am primarily responsible for hanger packaging and illustrating fun characters for our new baby and kids hangers. Each set of hangers was completed within a day.
