Subscription Boxes, E-mail Blasts, Branding, and Brochure Design

A Variety of Goods and Services

Packaging, Branding, Digital, Print
2015-2017

As a designer at MSI Media Group, a branding agency, I worked with the senior designer on packaging for Loacker's subscription boxes. Loacker is a chocolate company very popular in Italy and is pretty well known in the U.S. for their fresh ingredients. The senior designer created the front of the box while I created the sides and bottom.

I also worked on e-mail blasts with the marketing team in MSI Media Group. Mary's Secret Ingredients was a subscription box service that delivered curated cooking ingredients and goods to be used in recipes created by the founder. Mary would send out recipes of the week to subscribers. My graphics for these recipes were featured on these e-blasts as well as promotions to buy the next season's box (which were created by another designer).

Before designing at MSI Media Group, I studied graphic design at Baruch College and worked on projects that included creating logos for countries such as Andorra. Andorra is a small country between Spain and France known for its mountainous terrain and tourism for skiing. Afterwards, I moved onto JWC Real Estate Group (formerly known as ReMax Midtown) and designed brochures such as the Kelly+Chang booklet featuring top projects.
